Welcome to My Site

Here we have a bunch of fun things to look at, the main thing being butterflies. They happen to be my favorite thing and the main part of this website. I also chose the colors pink and green because my favorite color is pink and it's always nice to have a green thumb. I am a huge nature nerd and I have a weird affinity for butterflies in general. I love Queen Alexandra's Birdwing butterfly because they look like they're from a fantasy. They are so incredibly huge that they look absolutely fake. In fact I posted a picture of it below so everyone can see how huge it is.

Another one I think is really pretty is the Emporer butterfly. It's got shiny blue wings and I think you see it the best at night. I've seen one in person before actually and have my own pictures of it. It's wings are super shiny. I love about that butterfly is the blue coloring on it.

There is another type of emporer that is purple and it's a little smaller than the blue emporer butterfly. I love the wings.
